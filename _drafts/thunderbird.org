** How to Integrate Google Calendar Into Thunderbird
   :PROPERTIES:
   :CUSTOM_ID: how-to-integrate-google-calendar-into-thunderbird
   :END:

[[https://www.makeuseof.com/tag/how-to-integrate-google-calendar-into-thunderbird/][How
to Integrate Google Calendar Into Thunderbird]]

[[https://www.makeuseof.com/tag/how-to-integrate-google-calendar-into-thunderbird/][Google
Calendar integration requires two Thunderbird add-ons]]:

- *Lightning* (pre-packaged as of Thunderbird 38) adds a Sunbird-type
  calendar to Thunderbird.
- *Provider for Google Calendar* connects Lightning with Google Calendar
  for bi-directional read and write access.
