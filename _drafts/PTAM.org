* Introduction
  :PROPERTIES:
  :CUSTOM_ID: introduction
  :END:

PTAM (Parallel Tracking and Mapping) is a camera tracking system for
augmented reality. It requires no markers, pre-made maps, known
templates, or inertial sensors. If you're unfamiliar with PTAM have a
look at some [[http://www.robots.ox.ac.uk/~gk/youtube.html][videos]]
made with PTAM.

** Calibrate Camera
   :PROPERTIES:
   :CUSTOM_ID: calibrate-camera
   :END:

1. Modify cameracalibrator.launc

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
    yubao@yubao-Z370M-S01:~/catkin_ws/src/ethzasl_ptam/ptam$ cat launch/cameracalibrator.launch
    <launch>
        <node name="cameracalibrator" pkg="ptam" type="cameracalibrator" clear_params="true" output="screen">
            <remap from="image" to="/usb_cam/image_raw" />
                <remap from="pose" to="pose"/>
            <rosparam file="$(find ptam)/PtamFixParams.yaml"/>
        </node>
    </launch>
#+END_SRC

1. Run cameracalibrator.launch

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
    yubao@yubao-Z370M-S01:~/catkin_ws/src/ethzasl_ptam$ roslaunch ptam cameracalibrator.launch
#+END_SRC

2. Start usb_cam_node

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
    yubao@yubao-Z370M-S01:~/catkin_ws/src/ethzasl_ptam$ rosrun  usb_cam usb_cam_node
#+END_SRC
